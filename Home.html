About The Hostel

Kameng hostel has been a home away from home for students of IIT Guwahati since 2005. Named after a tributary of the Brahmaputra, Kameng has over the years proved to be one of the best hostels in terms of performance in IIT Guwahati’s cultural, technical and sports competitions. Having won both Manthan, the intra college cultural competition, and Spardha, the inter college sports competition, Kameng is also the proud winner of the General Championship Trophy. Fear feeling homesick? Worry not, the lawns in every block, the ever helpful seniors and the friendly mess staff will make you feel right at home. Kameng also houses a canteen that stays open till 2 A.M and a juice center that stays open till 1 A.M to satisfy your midnight cravings.
